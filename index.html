About Us

PEZT POLIZEI, INC. offers the comprehensive solution for your facility or home against the common household pests; and pathogens such as viruses and bacteria. We are a full service company that is committed in providing a pest control program against the common pests such as: Cockroaches, Mosquitoes, Flies, Termites, Ants, Bedbugs, Ticks, and Rodents. We also provide disinfection and sanitation of facilities against the dreaded Corona virus (Covid19). Established in 2016, our office is located at the Ground Floor of Jaysons Building, 12 National Highway, Brgy. Putatan, Muntinlupa City. We are a corporation duly registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission and existing under the Philippine Laws.
